About this template

It’s a small, modern landing page with a hero, features, an about block, and a contact form. Edit the colors at the top of the CSS to instantly reskin the site.

Want darker or lighter? Your system theme is respected, thanks to prefers-color-scheme.

Quick tips

  • Change the logo color by editing --brand.
  • Replace the hero panel with an actual image.
  • Duplicate a .card to add more content.
